🚗 Electric vs Gasoline Vehicles

This dataset contains 2025 vehicles. In this activity, you will compare Electric and Gasoline vehicles using fuel efficiency and environmental rating.

Focus Question:
How do Electric vehicles compare to Gasoline vehicles in fuel efficiency and environmental impact?

Step 1: Explore the Dataset

  • Scroll across the table to see all column names.
  • Find the column Primary Fuel Type.
  • Notice which vehicles are labeled Electric and which are Gasoline.
  • Look at the columns for City Fuel Efficiency and Highway Fuel Efficiency.

Step 2: Compare Fuel Efficiency

  • Drag City Fuel Efficiency (MPG/MPGe) to the x-axis.
  • What is the highest value you see?
  • Click on a dot to see which vehicle it represents in the table.

Step 3: Separate by Fuel Type

  • Drag Primary Fuel Type to the y-axis to create separate dotplots.
  • Do Electric vehicles tend to have higher city fuel efficiency than Gasoline vehicles?
  • Use the ruler icon to compare the mean or median for each group.

Step 4: Investigate Environmental Rating

  • Replace the x-axis with Environmental Rating (1–10).
  • Which fuel type tends to have higher ratings?
  • Are there any overlaps between Electric and Gasoline vehicles?

Step 5: Explore Vehicle Class

  • Drag Vehicle Class to the center of the graph to color the points.
  • Are Electric vehicles more common in certain vehicle classes?
