🚗 Electric vs Gasoline Vehicles

This dataset contains 2025 vehicles. In this activity, you will compare Electric and Gasoline vehicles using fuel efficiency, annual fuel cost, and environmental rating.

Focus Question:
How do Electric vehicles compare to Gasoline vehicles in cost, efficiency, and environmental impact?

Step 1: Explore the Dataset

  • Scroll across the table to see all column names.
  • Find the column Primary Fuel Type.
  • Notice which vehicles are labeled Electric and which are Gasoline.

Step 2: Investigate Annual Fuel Cost

  • Drag Estimated Annual Fuel Cost ($) to the x-axis.
  • What is the highest annual fuel cost you see?
  • Click on a dot to see which vehicle it represents.

Step 3: Compare by Fuel Type

  • Drag Primary Fuel Type to the y-axis to create separate graphs.
  • Which fuel type appears to have lower annual fuel costs?

Step 4: Explore Environmental Rating

  • Replace the x-axis with Environmental Rating (1–10).
  • Which fuel type tends to have higher ratings?
  • Use the ruler icon to view summary statistics (mean or median).
